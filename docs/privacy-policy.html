Privacy Policy

Marina Del Rey Sailing Weather  ·  Published by Foley Mobile Apps  ·  Effective: March 18, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the Marina Del Rey Sailing Weather Android app (package: com.sailingweather), developed and published by Foley Mobile Apps, handles information when you use the app.

What We Do Not Collect

This app does not:

  • Require an account or any form of login
  • Collect your name, email address, or any personally identifiable information
  • Access your contacts, camera, microphone, or photos
  • Store any personal data on device or on our own servers
  • Display advertisements or share data with ad networks
  • Use any third-party SDKs other than Firebase (Google)

What Is Collected Automatically (Firebase)

The app uses Firebase Analytics and Firebase Crashlytics, services provided by Google LLC. These services automatically collect limited, non-personal technical information to help us understand how the app is used and to diagnose crashes. This includes:

  • Device model and manufacturer
  • Android OS version
  • App version
  • General, anonymous usage events (e.g., app opens, screen views)
  • Crash reports and stack traces

This information is collected anonymously. No data is linked to your identity. Google's handling of this data is governed by the Google Privacy Policy.

Weather Data

The app fetches current weather conditions from external sources over the internet. These requests do not include any personal information beyond what is standard in an HTTP request (such as your IP address, which is processed by the remote server and not stored by us).

Data Retention

Foley Mobile Apps does not store any user data. Crash and analytics data retained by Firebase is subject to Google's own data retention policies, which you can review at firebase.google.com/support/privacy.

Children's Privacy

This app is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children. If you believe a child has provided information through this app, please contact us so we can address the situation.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted at this URL with an updated effective date. Continued use of the app after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
